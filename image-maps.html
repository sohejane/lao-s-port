Paris

Paris is worth visiting for its romantic vibes, splendid boulevards, cool nightlife, and top-notch fashion boutiques.
Architecture geeks will love the breathtaking Eiffel Tower; nature admirers – the Tuileries Gardens; food fans – the world-class cuisine, and culture vultures – the Louvre.
